Charm City Current

Visit archives

Charm City Current was created for the Baltimore Sun Media Group to collect the best blogging the city had to offer.

During its successful two-year run, the site acted as a platform for top voices -- from an art museum director to an up-and-coming band -- to share their perspectives. The site was built on a WordPress MU instance, with custom plugin logic for display of the latest fresh content.

Role
WordPress Plugin authoring, setup, maintenance
