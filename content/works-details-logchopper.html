LogChopper

Visit online

A few years ago, a friend and I saw a developer need.

A popular browser -- we won't say which one -- was crashing whenever developers ran code with uncommented "console.log()" calls..

Out of that frustration came LogChopper. The tool deletes logging calls or wraps them in comment tags, making them harmless.
